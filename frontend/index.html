About This Collection

DevBubba's Bookmarklet Collection is a comprehensive, carefully curated collection of bookmarklets designed to enhance your browsing experience and boost productivity. We focus on providing high-quality, useful tools that have been thoroughly reviewed and tested. All bookmarklets are organized by category to make discovery easy and intuitive.

Whether you're a developer seeking productivity tools, a student looking to enhance your workflow, or a power user who loves customizing the web, you'll find valuable tools in our collection. Each bookmarklet comes with detailed descriptions, clear usage instructions, and status indicators to help you find exactly what you need.

Report an Issue

Found a bug or have a suggestion for improvement? We value your feedback and want to make this collection as useful as possible. Use our issue reporting system to let us know about any problems you encounter or ideas you have for new features.

When reporting an issue, please include as much detail as possible, such as the bookmarklet name, what you expected to happen, what actually happened, and steps to reproduce the issue. This helps us quickly identify and fix problems.
